How to get prescription

Whether you're getting a new prescription or renewing an expired one, take care of it virtually or get an in-person eye exam.

Book a comprehensive eye exam

  • need an up-to-date prescription (even if it's your first)
  • wear any glasses or contact lens prescription
  • have eye health concerns, including headaches or vision changes
  • are over the age of 4

Renew it from home in just 10 minutes

  • see well with their current glasses or contacts
  • have a single-vision distance prescription
  • have no eye health concerns
  • are between 18 and 65 years old
  • reside in an eligible state. Due to state-level regulations, these states are currently ineligible to use Virtual Vision Test: AK, CT, DC, GA, ID, KY, NJ, NM, SC, SD, WA, WV
